What to include in your message

New Enquiry — info@nerofylabs.com

Email us your name and requirements and we'll get back to you with a tailored recommendation. To help us advise you best, please include:

  • Your name and suburb — so we know where you're based and can plan an installation visit.
  • Your main water concern — drinking water quality, skin and hair protection, or whole-home filtration.
  • Your home type — house, townhouse or apartment, and the number of bathrooms if relevant.
  • Any questions — about specific products, pricing, installation timelines, or maintenance requirements.

Not sure what you need? That's completely fine — just describe your situation and we'll guide you to the right solution.
